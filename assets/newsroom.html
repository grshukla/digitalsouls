Equations PR & Media Founder Priyanka Bhatt awarded by PRMoment for ‘30 under 30’

Mumbai, February 27, 2015: It was indeed a proud moment for Priyanka Bhatt, Founder and CEO, Equations PR & Media who has been awarded recently by PRMoment amongst the top 30 PR professionals in India. The first ever contest run by PRmoment.in (an online magazine for the PR fraternity) in association with Adfactors PR, called for all India entries from PR professionals across the country who have done some exceptional work in just under 30 years of age. The jury panel consisted of some eminent stalwarts from the PR industry namely Madan Bahal, MD, Adfactors; Valerie Pinto, CEO, Weber Shandwick; Nikhil Dey, President, Genesis Burson-Marstellar; RakeshThukral, MD, Edelman India and many more to pick the best out of around hundred entries that were received. Conducted in the month of December, 2014, PRmoment announced winners on 12th February, 2015.
